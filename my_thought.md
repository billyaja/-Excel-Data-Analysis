## Overview
This project provides an integrated analysis of AtliQ, a company specializing in hardware and peripherals sales through Brick & Mortar (e.g., Croma, Best Buy) and E-commerce (e.g., Amazon, Flipkart) platforms, utilizing Retailer, Direct, and Distributor channels. By combining **sales and financial performance reports**, this analysis delivers a holistic view of AtliQ's operations. By combining sales and financial performance reports, this analysis delivers a holistic view of AtliQ's operations from 2019 to 2021.

**The Sales Report** aims to: 
1. create a [customer performance report](/Customer%20Performance.pdf) to identify valuable customers, 

2. compare [market performance](/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target.pdf) against sales targets to pinpoint improvement areas, and 
![market performance](/assets/Net%20Sales.jpeg)
3. track sales KPIs to optimize customer discounts and explore expansion opportunities.

**The Finance Report** seeks to: 
1. Focus on creating [Profit and Loss (P&L) reports segmented by Fiscal Year](/P&L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf) and [Markets](/P&L%20Statement%20by%20Markets.pdf). 
2. Evaluate financial performance, support decision-making, and facilitate communication with stakeholders.

## Data and Methodology
- Data Scale and Scope:
    - The dataset comprises approximately **800k rows** of sales and financial data, providing a robust foundation for in-depth analysis.
- ETL Methodology:
    - Proficient in **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)** methodology, ensuring data is properly extracted from diverse sources, transformed to meet analytical requirements, and loaded into a structured data model
- Date Table Generation:
    - Expertise in generating **date tables using Power Query** to create a structured time dimension, crucial for time-based analysis and reporting [from conversation history].
    - Capable of deriving **fiscal months and quarters** to align with AtliQ's specific financial reporting periods, ensuring accurate financial insights 
- Data Modeling with Power Pivot:
    - Skilled in establishing **data model relationships using Power Pivot**, enabling the creation of a relational data model that supports complex queries and analysis
![data_model](/assets/Data%20Model.png)
- DAX Calculations:
    - Skilled in utilizing DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) to create calculated columns and metrics, enabling the derivation of new KPIs and in-depth financial and sales performance metrics
